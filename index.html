Overview

Hyper Rush follows a young female runner named Kali, as she tries to find her footing in the tech conglomerate based city called Los Techno’s. She is young, beautiful, and full of fire, which is why she was chosen as a runner for her upcoming job. She is tasked with delivering a time sensitive package outside Los Techno’s in the Drydata wasteland. During the time of her run, plans change and the drop off location is updated, but the navigation is too slow to react. She is forced to take a quick ‘sho’t left’, causing the car to drift and the contents of the time sensitive package to be expelled from its container.

Genre – Retro, Cyberpunk.

Target Audience – Fans of retro movies and films like ‘Back to the Future’ and ‘Blade Runner’ and an audience age between twenty to forty.

Distribution – 25 minute TV episodes and full series on Netflix, Amazon Prime and/or Disney Plus.

  • Major conflict is the updating of the drop off zone as well as the late navigation change.
  • Key plot points are her putting lipstick on, constantly looking at the package, the navigation updating, the strawberries flying out the container and the car drifting.
  • Kali doesn’t have much of character arc besides staying calm in stressful situations, so it could be her ability to constantly adapt and overcome obstacles no matter what.

Characters:

Nightrider (Ford Shelby Mustang GT500)

  • Though simply a car it contains various aspects reflecting the driver Kali herself.
  • Midnight black accompanied by neon pink lights brins that light in the darkness feel for Kali, as well as showing her bright and quirky character.
  • The XOs on the car as well as the neon pink spider web, show her more dangerous side with reference to love and spiders, in which the female spider eats her male counterpart.
  • Lastly on the exterior of the vehicle is word ‘HOT’ which portrays her confidence and courage in the way she acts and looks.
  • The interior follows the same theme as the exterior, but with a retro twist, lots of buttons, dials and monitors to bring in that classic retro feel.
  • The car is all about style and smoothness which reflects Kali’s personality. Kali (The main girl of the block)
  • Kali is young ex-net runner who comes from a wealthy crime family.
  • She’s determined to make it on her own without the help of her family, which is why she runs a job like this.
  • She very gothic and dark which shows with her various arm tattoos and midnight black fingernails.
  • She also adorns lipstick and make up as she loves the aesthetic and makes her feel empowered.
  • Lastly she has a bad girl attitude and is always cool under pressure no matter the situation. (Boss girl mentality)

Concept Art

Artist

Anxiety^2 is a young inspiring duo looking to bring back the classic feeling of nostalgia and retro feel into the modern era, with 80s inspired animations and drawings. We are all about having fun and creating something cool and retro. First up is Matthew Benjamin Hunter who does 3D modeling, animation, and mechanical drawings. He is fun, quirky, hardworking, and reliable, which shows in his work as he modeled the interior and animated the car. Lastly but certainly not least, the rock and the brains behind the group Athena Nicole Naidoo. She does 3D modeling, 2D/3D animation as well as drawing. She is creative, determined and always wiling to try her best.
